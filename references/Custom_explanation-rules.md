## Scope

This rule applies only to the following components.

*  explanation
***

### ✅ Good Examples (Meet all evaluation criteria)

*   Click the calendar below and select a date.
*   Enter the required information and click the “Register” button.
*   Review the displayed content before proceeding with the update.
*   Drag and drop the file to upload it.
*   Set the conditions and click the search button.

**Points:**

*   Uses polite and clear instructional tone
*   Every sentence **ends with a verb** (e.g., select, click, review)
*   **Avoids nominal sentences** (e.g., “input,” “confirmation” without verbs)

***

### ❌ Bad Examples (Violating one or more criteria)

*   Selection of the calendar below.
*   After entering required information, the register button.
*   Confirmation of displayed content.
*   File upload.
*   After setting conditions, execution of search.

**Issues:**

*   **Missing verbs** (nominal sentence endings)
*   Not written in a clear instructional tone
*   Instructions are incomplete and insufficient for guiding users

***

### Summary (Checklist)

When writing descriptions, make sure to:

*   ✅ Use a polite and clear instructional tone
*   ✅ **Always include a verb** (e.g., “please click,” “select”)
*   ✅ **Avoid nominal sentence endings**
